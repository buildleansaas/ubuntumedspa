## Why sterile single-use equipment matters

Blomdahl uses sterile single-use disposable cassettes. That matters because the part of the system that touches the ear is not reused between patients.

For parents, this is easy to understand: if you are getting a child's ears pierced, you want fewer variables, not more. Sterile single-use equipment is one of the cleanest decision points in the comparison.
