# Pediatric-Aware Ear Piercing in Williamsburg, VA

Parents are not only choosing where earrings are placed. They are choosing who talks their child through the visit, who answers aftercare questions, and what system is used for starter jewelry.

Jenny Coleman, MSN, RN, CPNP, PMHS brings pediatric nursing experience to [Blomdahl ear piercing](/procedures/blomdahl-ear-piercing) at Williamsburg Med Spa.

## What pediatric awareness changes

A pediatric-aware appointment can help with:

- explaining each step in simple language,
- giving parents time for questions,
- reviewing comfort and timing,
- discussing jewelry material,
- making aftercare clear before the family leaves.

It does not mean every child is ready at every moment. It means the visit can be planned with the child's age, temperament, and parent concerns in mind.

## Why Blomdahl fits this approach

Blomdahl medical ear piercing uses sterile disposable piercing cassettes and hypoallergenic starter jewelry. For parents, that means the appointment can stay focused on clean technique, placement, and aftercare.

Blomdahl starter jewelry options include Medical Plastic and Medical Grade Titanium.

## Good questions to ask before a child's piercing

Before the visit, parents may want to ask:

- What starter jewelry is used?
- How should ears be cleaned after piercing?
- When can earrings be changed?
- What symptoms should prompt a call?
- Should we avoid sports, swimming, or travel right after?
