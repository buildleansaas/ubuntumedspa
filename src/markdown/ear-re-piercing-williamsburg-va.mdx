# Ear Re-Piercing in Williamsburg, VA

Closed or partially closed ear piercings are common. The safest next step is not to force jewelry through the old hole. It is to have the area reviewed before deciding where and whether to pierce again.

Williamsburg Med Spa offers [ear re-piercing](/procedures/blomdahl-ear-piercing/for/re-piercing) with Blomdahl medical ear piercing.

## Why placement review matters

Old holes, scar tissue, uneven placement, irritation, or a history of keloids can affect the plan. Sometimes the best spot is not the exact old location.

Jenny can review the area and talk through what makes sense before piercing.

## Why Blomdahl can be useful for re-piercing

Blomdahl uses sterile disposable piercing cassettes and hypoallergenic starter jewelry options. For re-piercing, that can support a clean, structured appointment with clear aftercare.

## Do not force jewelry through

If the hole has narrowed or closed, forcing jewelry through can irritate tissue or create a rough channel. Schedule a review instead.
