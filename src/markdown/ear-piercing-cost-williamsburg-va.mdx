# Ear Piercing Cost in Williamsburg, VA

Ear piercing cost should be understood in context. The cheapest option is not always the best fit when you care about sterile equipment, starter jewelry material, provider experience, and aftercare.

Williamsburg Med Spa lists [Blomdahl ear piercing](/procedures/blomdahl-ear-piercing) at $45 per ear.

## What should cost include?

When comparing ear piercing options, ask what is included:

- starter jewelry,
- sterile disposable piercing products,
- placement discussion,
- provider time,
- aftercare instructions,
- support if questions come up afterward.

## Why medical-grade piercing may cost more

Blomdahl medical ear piercing uses sterile disposable piercing cassettes and hypoallergenic starter jewelry options. At Williamsburg Med Spa, the visit is appointment-based with Jenny Coleman, MSN, RN, CPNP, PMHS.

That structure is different from a quick walk-in retail piercing.

## Questions to ask before booking

Before scheduling, ask:

- Is pricing per ear or for both ears?
- What starter jewelry is used?
- Are aftercare supplies included?
- Can parents ask questions before a child's piercing?
- What happens if there is a concern during healing?
