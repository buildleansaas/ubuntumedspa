# Hypoallergenic Ear Piercing for Sensitive Skin

If earrings have made your ears itchy, red, or irritated in the past, you may be looking for hypoallergenic ear piercing instead of a standard retail option.

Williamsburg Med Spa offers [Blomdahl Ear Piercing](/procedures/blomdahl-ear-piercing), which includes medical plastic and titanium starter jewelry options. Sensitive-ear patients can also review the dedicated [hypoallergenic ear piercing page](/procedures/blomdahl-ear-piercing/for/sensitive-ears).

## Why nickel concerns matter

Nickel sensitivity is one reason some people react to jewelry. Reactions can vary, and irritation is not always caused by nickel, but starter jewelry material is still worth taking seriously.

If you have a known allergy or past reaction, mention it before the piercing appointment.

## Medical plastic and titanium starter jewelry

Blomdahl starter jewelry options include hypoallergenic medical plastic and titanium. These materials are selected for people who want a careful first-piercing or re-piercing experience.

The right option depends on age, placement, sensitivity history, and provider guidance.

## Sensitive skin aftercare

Sensitive ears need consistent aftercare. Follow the instructions given at your appointment and avoid unnecessary touching, twisting, or switching jewelry too early.

Call the clinic or seek medical advice if you notice worsening redness, swelling, drainage, warmth, fever, or pain that feels unusual.

## Who may benefit from a medical-grade setting?

Hypoallergenic piercing may be a good fit for:

- children getting first earrings,
- adults with sensitive ears,
- people with nickel concerns,
- people re-piercing closed holes,
- families who want a quiet appointment and clear instructions.
