# Blomdahl Ear Piercing in Williamsburg, VA

Blomdahl is a medical ear piercing system designed around sterile disposable piercing products, hypoallergenic starter jewelry, and a provider-led process.

## What makes Blomdahl different?

Blomdahl's ear piercing system is built around modern aseptic technique. The system uses sterile disposable piercing cassettes, which helps keep the visit clean and organized.

The starter jewelry is also part of the system. Blomdahl offers Medical Plastic and Medical Grade Titanium options, and Blomdahl describes its Medical Plastic as 0% nickel.
