# Ear Piercing Aftercare After a Blomdahl Piercing

Good aftercare helps protect the result of a clean piercing visit. Your exact instructions should come from your provider, but the main idea is simple: keep the area clean, avoid unnecessary touching, and do not change starter earrings too early.

Williamsburg Med Spa reviews aftercare during each [Blomdahl ear piercing appointment](/procedures/blomdahl-ear-piercing).

## Keep starter earrings in place

Blomdahl aftercare guidance generally keeps starter earrings in place during the early healing window. For earlobes, that commonly means about six weeks before changing earrings.

Do not change earrings early unless Jenny gives you different instructions.

## Keep hands away

Touching new earrings with unwashed hands can irritate the area. Children may need reminders not to twist, pull, or play with new earrings.

## Watch for concerns

Call the clinic or seek medical advice if you notice:

- worsening redness,
- swelling,
- warmth,
- drainage,
- fever,
- increasing pain,
- an earring that feels embedded or too tight.

## Plan around activities

If a child has sports, swimming, travel, or a major event coming up, mention that before scheduling. Timing matters when you want aftercare to be manageable.
