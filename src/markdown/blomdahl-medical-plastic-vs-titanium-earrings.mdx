# Blomdahl Medical Plastic vs Titanium Starter Earrings

For sensitive ears, the starter earring matters as much as the piercing itself. Starter earrings stay in while the piercing begins to heal, so parents and adults should understand the material before booking.

Williamsburg Med Spa uses [Blomdahl medical ear piercing](/procedures/blomdahl-ear-piercing) with hypoallergenic starter jewelry options, including Medical Plastic and Medical Grade Titanium.

## Short answer

Blomdahl Medical Plastic is the cleanest choice when nickel concern is the main issue because Blomdahl describes it as 0% nickel. Medical Grade Titanium is also a strong hypoallergenic option for many patients who prefer a metal starter earring. Jenny can review the available options at the appointment.

## Why starter earrings matter

The first earrings are not just decorative. They are part of the healing process. If the material irritates the ear, the piercing can become harder to care for, especially for children who may touch or twist earrings when they feel itchy.

That is why Williamsburg Med Spa uses Blomdahl starter jewelry for initial piercings rather than outside jewelry.

## When Medical Plastic may be the better fit

Medical Plastic may be the better fit when:

- nickel sensitivity is the biggest concern,
- a child has reacted to earrings before,
- parents want the lowest-nickel starter option available in the Blomdahl system,
- the patient wants a lightweight starter earring,
- this is a first piercing and the family wants fewer material variables.

For this path, review [hypoallergenic ear piercing for sensitive ears](/procedures/blomdahl-ear-piercing/for/sensitive-ears).

## When titanium may be the better fit

Medical Grade Titanium may be the better fit when:

- the patient prefers a metal starter earring,
- prior reactions are mild or uncertain,
- the goal is a hypoallergenic metal option,
- the patient is a teen or adult who already knows titanium works well for them.

Titanium is not a reason to skip the conversation. If you have a known metal allergy, tell Jenny before choosing starter earrings.

## What parents should ask before booking

Before a child's first piercing, ask:

- Which Blomdahl starter earring options are currently available?
- Is Medical Plastic or titanium the better starting point for my child?
- How long should starter earrings stay in?
- What should we avoid during healing?
- When should we call if the ear looks irritated?

Those questions are exactly why an appointment-based visit can be useful.
